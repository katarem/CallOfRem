TNT - high-res textures and flats
-----------------------------------


Textures-8.exe and Textures-9.exe contain most of the final Doom TNT textures. 

Purpose
-------
There is nothing in this for me other than trying to make a worthwhile contribution to the 
Doom community by enabling these resources to be more available to people with restricted 
bandwidth.
